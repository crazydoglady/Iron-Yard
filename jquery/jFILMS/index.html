Erika was born in the warmth of Florida, raised in Wisconsin winters. She graduated from University of Wisconsin-Madison with a BA in Communications. After a few years in the corporate world thwarting would-be frauds she followed her dreams to FSU and earned her MFA in Film Production, Direction, Writing and Editing.

Since then, Erika has worked with a variety of media outlets including CBS, ESPN, and ABC. She has been affiliated with nationally syndicated programs such as the Steve Harvey show as well as web productions for Yahoo!. Her professional career has also included work in the areas of print media, advertisement layout and as faculty in Secondary Education.

Currently, Erika is employed with 2C Media in addition to owning and operating Lower Case j, a company specializing in pitching, writing, directing, and editing television and online commercials for regional clients. Lower Case j will is also experienced in projects for narrative films, web content, BVs (BizVids), and personal video projects. Specialties include: Micro-budget filmmaking, pitching, story conceptualization, and marketing

When Erika is engrossed in editing a film reel at 2am she may be found basking in the Miami sun, enjoying a glass of wine, discussing Star Wars with her brother or rescuing unwanted houseplants and orchids from the side of the road
